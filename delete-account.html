How to delete your account

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at chetverikovevgenii@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".

What data will be deleted

  • Your account credentials (email, UID)
  • All collaborative checklists and their items

What data we may retain

We may retain anonymized or aggregated data for analytics purposes. All personal data is deleted within 30 days of the request.
